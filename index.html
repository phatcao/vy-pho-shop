Mr.Pho Vietnamese Restaurant

Around Hoan Kiem Lake from 1954 to now, there is only one noodle shop. That is Mr.Pho at house number 61, Dinh Tien Hoang street, located at the entrance of some households. In 1955, Mr. Pho decided to open a pho restaurant at 61 Dinh Tien Hoang, opposite Ngoc Son Temple.

MR. PHO 1955 (The oldest pho restaurant at 61 Dinh Tien Hoang, Hanoi)

Over the past 60 years, although the city has gone through a few changes, the shop is still there, not much different from the original.
Now few young people can understand the value of the oldest pho restaurant in Hanoi. That place is not only the memories, the nostalgia but also the heart of Mr. Bui Tri Thin who built Mr. Pho is famous in Hanoi. To preserve and develop his brand value, now Mr. Bui Chi Thanh, who is his true grandson, is trying to keep the family recipe every day to make delicious bowls of pho for tourists to enjoy. Because Mr. Pho was so famous at that time that there were also a few shops named after him. However, now Mr. Pho Bo Ho is available at 61 Dinh Tien Hoang.

OUR CUSTOMERS

Each bowl of Pho Thin is quite expensive, 60,000 VND for a full bowl, but every day, hundreds of visitors both Western and Vietnamese still flock to it. It can be said that in Hanoi, there are not so many attractive old restaurants.
Especially in Pho Thin, the bowl of pho is inherently "cheap", those who don't like the greasy, only visit once to say, who likes it, come back with it, to relieve the craving for sad Hanoi mornings.
The shop is small, cramped, hot summer of all kinds, but the strange thing in the "miserable" eating habits of Hanoi is that, the culture of waiting in line to enjoy a delicious bowl of pho.
